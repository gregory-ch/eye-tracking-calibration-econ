Interface Guide

Cell information:

  • Decision number: top-left corner
  • Decision value: bottom-right corner
  • Previous answer: top-right of the screen
  • Signal ball: centre of the reserved cell (press ↑ to reveal)

Controls reminder:

  • ← → : Adjust value
  • Hold arrows: Fast adjustment
  • Enter: Confirm decision
  • ↑ : Show signal ball (second screen)
