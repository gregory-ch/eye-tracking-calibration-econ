Game Instructions

Game Structure

In this game, the asset value can be either 0 or 100. You will see decisions made by previous robots who were following the same rules and had their own signals.

The game consists of two screens:

  • First screen: Shows history of previous decisions
  • Second screen: Shows the same history plus a signal (red or blue ball)
Signal Distribution
  • If asset value is 100:
    • 70% chance of red ball
    • 30% chance of blue ball
  • If asset value is 0:
    • 30% chance of red ball
    • 70% chance of blue ball
Controls
  • Use ← and → arrow keys to adjust your value
  • Hold arrows for faster adjustment
  • Press Enter to confirm your decision
  • Hold ↓ to view detailed instructions at any time
  • Press ↑ to reveal your signal ball on the second screen
Decision Process
  • Make your first decision based on the history
  • On the second screen, you'll see:
    • Your first decision (top-right corner)
    • A signal ball (in the reserved cell)
    • You can make your final decision

Press Enter to start
